02 / Studio note

A note from Katsuko, New York

We started Katsuko because these products looked unrelated only from a distance. A running race, a daily check-in, and a cruise decision each ask the same question: how can technology help without crowding out agency, dignity, or imagination? We build from New York, keeping the studio small enough to stay close to the details and honest about what each product is ready to promise. Katsuko is the thread between them: useful systems, considered constraints, and room for people to remain more than inputs.
